Where it all Began:
The Origin

OVM CLO was born in 2022 as OVM ORIGINALS from a simple belief: style should carry meaning, identity, and intention. What began as a vision to create refined streetwear quickly became a pursuit of timeless design.

Each piece blends minimalist-inspired structure with classic aesthetics, creating garments that feel both modern and enduring. OVM CLO represents confidence, culture, and a commitment to understated luxury.
